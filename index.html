About Us

Serving Cary, NC and the surrounding areas for almost 30 years.

Cary was a small railroad town and Apex was half farmland in the early 90s. Authentic Japanese groceries and gifts were hard to find.

That is when Sachiko decided to bring Toyo Shokuhin and Gifts to life.

Later Midori began working at Toyo Shokuhin. For many years Midori helped operate Toyo Shokuhin together with Sachiko. After almost 30 years of operation, Sachiko reluctantly sold the business to Midori to continue the legacy. This year, in 2021, the transition begins. We hope to keep the "old way of doing business" while also updating some things to provide you, the customer, the best experience.

Why Toyo?

Because our goal is not to sell products. We want you to experience a piece of Japan.
